Get ready to experience the world like never before.

Vista VR is an upcoming Virtual Reality application that will allow users to travel the world and explore real-life locations around the world with their friends, in real-time. Travel the world and go hiking in the Himalayas, traverse the Sahara desert, discover the wonders of the Amazon, or even take a tour of London. Every day can be a vacation with Vista VR.

On top of getting to visit places from all over the world, you'll also get a workout in doing so. Vista VR will actively monitor and track calories burned, distance traveled, and will even remind you when it's time to take a drink break. Working out has never been this fun before.
